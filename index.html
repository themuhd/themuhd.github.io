About Me

Hello there! I'm Muhammed, a data scientist and enthusiast with a strong technical background and a passion for solving real-world problems with data. I'm currently pursuing my Master's degree in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at the University of Salford. I'm particularly interested in the areas of natural language processing, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. I believe that these technologies have the potential to revolutionize the way we interact with the world around us.I'm interested in using data science to help businesses and organizations reduce their carbon footprint and achieve net zero emissions. I also believe that data science can be used to develop new and innovative applications for cryptocurrency, such as using it to make micropayments or to track the provenance of goods.

My goal is to make valuable contributions in the future to the field of data science by building my enterprise, focusing on mentoring the next generation of data scientists and making well-informed decisions by application of information analysis and statistical techniques to increase business value.

>
I'm also passionate about painting and contemporary art.I enjoy playing football and tennis in my spare time. I've created this portfolio to showcase my work and share my passion for data science. I hope you enjoy exploring it!
